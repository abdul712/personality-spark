Introduction

The twin flame runner's internal emotional landscape represents one of the most complex psychological phenomena in spiritual relationships. Behind the external behavior of distancing and avoidance lies a rich tapestry of deep feelings that are often misunderstood by both the runner themselves and their twin flame counterpart. These feelings emerge from the collision between the soul's recognition of its other half and the ego's desperate attempt to maintain familiar patterns of self-protection.
