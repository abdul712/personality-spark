Anger

Anger, a natural and powerful emotion triggered by feelings of threat, frustration, or hurt, can have a profound impact on our mental and physical well-being. When we experience anger, it can be difficult to navigate the complex emotions that arise. If you find yourself feeling angry after receiving a late-night call from your ex, it's important to acknowledge and understand your emotions.

It's important to validate your feelings and understand that anger itself is not negative, but rather the actions taken in response to anger can have positive or negative consequences.
