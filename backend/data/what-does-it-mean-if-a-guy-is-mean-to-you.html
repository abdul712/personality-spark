Manipulation Tactic

In the realm of power dynamics and control, the manipulation tactic serves as a strategic tool employed by individuals seeking to assert dominance over others. This tactic often involves gaslighting tactics, emotional manipulation, psychological warfare, and mind games. Through these methods, the manipulator aims to undermine the victim's confidence, perception of reality, and mental well-being. Gaslighting, for example, involves making the victim doubt their thoughts, feelings, and experiences, ultimately leading to a sense of powerlessness. Emotional manipulation plays on the victim's emotions, coercing them into certain behaviors or responses. Psychological warfare and mind games are used to confuse and control the victim, creating a dynamic where the manipulator holds all the power.
