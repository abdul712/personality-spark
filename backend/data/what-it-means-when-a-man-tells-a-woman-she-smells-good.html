Another reason for the compliment could be the importance of fragrance in attracting attention and signaling personal identity. Fragrance has the power to leave a lasting impression and can be a way for individuals to differentiate themselves. Memory association is another factor that may prompt a man to compliment a woman's fragrance. The scent can evoke nostalgic or fond memories, and when a man recognizes a familiar aroma, he may feel compelled to express his appreciation.

Lastly, a man may simply like the fragrance and genuinely want to compliment the woman. It can be a sincere gesture of appreciation for the pleasant smell that the woman exudes.
