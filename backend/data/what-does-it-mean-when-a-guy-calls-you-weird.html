In this discussion, we will explore the various possibilities, from playful flirtation to subtle criticism, and even delve into the impact of social media and gender stereotypes on our interpretations.

By examining non-verbal cues and considering individuality and self-confidence, we aim to shed light on what it truly means when a guy refers to you as 'weird.'
