Flirting Signals

Observing subtle cues and body language can provide valuable insights into whether someone is engaging in flirting or simply testing the waters in a social interaction. When trying to decipher if someone is flirting, consider the following:

  1. Eye Contact: Prolonged eye contact accompanied by a smile can be a strong indicator of romantic interest.
  2. Mirroring: If the person mirrors your gestures or posture, it may suggest they are trying to establish a connection.
  3. Touch: Light, casual touches on the arm or shoulder can signal flirtation and a desire for physical closeness.

Understanding these nonverbal cues can help you navigate social interactions and interpret subtle signs of interest or attraction.
