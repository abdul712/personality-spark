Hands behind the back is a gesture that signifies dominance, authority, and confidence. When someone holds their hands behind their back, it demonstrates a sense of control and power. By positioning their hands behind their back, individuals showcase their self-assurance and a lack of fear. This gesture implies that they feel secure in their authority and are unafraid to take charge. It also signifies an ability to exude control over their emotions, as the hands behind the back help to restrain any impulsive movements.

In situations of negotiation, conflict, or challenging conversations, people often adopt this gesture to assert their dominance and leadership. By keeping their hands behind their back, they maintain a composed and authoritative demeanor, projecting an image of strength and confidence.
