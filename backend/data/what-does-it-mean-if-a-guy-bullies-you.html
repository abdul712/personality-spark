Types of Bullying Tactics

What are the common types of bullying tactics employed by individuals engaging in such behavior? Bullying can manifest in various forms, each with its own detrimental impact on the victim. Here are five prevalent types of bullying tactics:

  • Verbal Bullying: This involves name-calling, insults, or derogatory remarks aimed at belittling the victim.
  • Physical Bullying: Physical aggression such as hitting, kicking, pushing, or any form of bodily harm constitutes physical bullying.
  • Social Bullying: Social exclusion, spreading rumors, or manipulating relationships to isolate the victim fall under this category.
  • Cyberbullying: Using digital platforms to harass, threaten, or embarrass someone, cyberbullying can have far-reaching consequences.
  • Emotional Bullying: This involves psychological manipulation, playing mind games, or undermining the victim's self-esteem.

Understanding these tactics is crucial in identifying and addressing bullying behavior promptly. Recognizing the signs and implementing appropriate coping mechanisms can help mitigate the impactful consequences of bullying on individuals.
