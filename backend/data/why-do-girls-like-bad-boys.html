Assertiveness

Assertiveness is a crucial skill that empowers individuals to effectively communicate their thoughts, feelings, and needs while maintaining respect for themselves and others. It is about finding the balance between being overly aggressive and passive. Assertive individuals have the ability to confidently express themselves, set boundaries, make decisions, and stand up for themselves without infringing upon the rights of others.

Assertiveness is not about being rude or dominating conversations; instead, it involves advocating for oneself in a respectful and direct manner, while also considering the perspectives of others. It is about using 'I' statements to convey feelings and needs, rather than placing blame or making assumptions. Additionally, assertive individuals actively listen to others, acknowledging their viewpoints and demonstrating empathy.
