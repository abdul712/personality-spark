Emotional Attachment

Developing a deep emotional attachment with someone involves a profound connection that goes beyond surface interactions. According to attachment theory, formulated by John Bowlby, emotional bonding is essential for human development and well-being. This theory suggests that individuals seek closeness to others to feel secure, safe, and supported. Emotional attachment is built over time through shared experiences, trust, and understanding. It involves feeling a sense of belonging and comfort with another person, fostering a deep connection that impacts one's emotions and behaviors.
