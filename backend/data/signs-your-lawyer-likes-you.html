Prompt Responses

Promptness in communication is a key indicator of a lawyer's favorable disposition towards a client. When assessing a lawyer's responsiveness, email etiquette plays a crucial role. A lawyer who likes their client is likely to respond promptly to emails, demonstrating professionalism and a commitment to keeping the client informed.

Furthermore, the attorney's availability for text messages can also indicate their level of interest in the client's case. Lawyers who are invested in their clients often make themselves accessible via text for quick queries or updates.

Clients should pay attention to how quickly their lawyer responds to their messages. If a lawyer consistently takes a long time to reply to emails or text messages, it may suggest a lack of prioritization of the client's needs. On the other hand, swift responses indicate a proactive approach and a genuine interest in the client's concerns. By observing these communication patterns, clients can gauge their lawyer's level of engagement and dedication to their case.
