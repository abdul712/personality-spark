Mixed Signals and Confusion

Navigating mixed signals and confusion can be a complex and challenging experience when transitioning from dating to being just friends. Seeking support from non-romantic friendships can also be beneficial during this transitional period. Talking to a close friend or confidant can provide perspective and guidance. Additionally, considering therapy can be helpful in navigating mixed signals and confusion. A therapist can offer insight and tools to navigate the complexities of these situations.

It is important to recognize that there can be various reasons why someone wants to be friends after dating. Timing, fear of commitment, and lack of assertiveness are just a few potential factors to consider. Each person's journey and decision-making process is unique. By approaching the situation with empathy and understanding, both parties can work towards maintaining a healthy and fulfilling friendship.
