One way to gain insight into a guy's sense of humor is by understanding the different types of humor he may enjoy. Here are some common types of humor that men often appreciate:

2. Dry or Deadpan Humor: This style of humor involves delivering jokes with a serious tone, often using sarcasm or understatement. Men who appreciate dry or deadpan humor may enjoy witty one-liners and clever wordplay.

3. Observational Humor: This type of humor finds comedy in everyday situations and keenly observes human behavior. Men who appreciate observational humor often have a knack for finding humor in the mundane and can make witty observations about the world around them.
