Long-distance relationships require a unique level of effort and commitment to maintain a strong connection. Increased communication, particularly through virtual dates, plays a crucial role in keeping the flame alive. In times of longing and separation, the willingness to engage in frequent and meaningful communication demonstrates a deep yearning for connection and closeness.
