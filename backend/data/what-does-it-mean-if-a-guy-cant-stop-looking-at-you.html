Signs of Attraction

When someone is attracted to another person, there are often subtle yet telling signs that can be observed in their body language and behavior. Body language plays a significant role in non-verbal communication, and it can reveal a lot about a person's feelings towards someone else.

One common sign of attraction is increased eye contact. When a guy can't stop looking at you, it may indicate that he is interested or drawn to you in some way. Sustained eye contact can convey a sense of connection and intimacy, showing that the person is focused on you and what you are saying or doing.

Moreover, dilated pupils can also be a sign of attraction. When someone is genuinely interested or attracted to another person, their pupils tend to dilate as a subconscious physiological response. This can be a subtle yet telling cue that the person is captivated by your presence.

Paying attention to these body language signals, especially prolonged eye contact and dilated pupils, can provide valuable insights into someone's feelings towards you.

Understanding his interest in your personality can provide insight into the depth of his feelings and the potential for a meaningful connection.
