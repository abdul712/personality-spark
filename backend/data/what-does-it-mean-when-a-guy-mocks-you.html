Understanding Mockery

Understanding the underlying motives and dynamics of mockery can shed light on why some individuals engage in this behavior and how it can impact relationships.   • **Recognizing signs of mockery**: It is important to identify signs of mockery, such as sarcastically imitating your voice or gestures, making fun of your appearance or choices, and laughing at things that aren't genuinely funny.
