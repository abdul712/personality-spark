    Understanding the subtle cues in his laughter can provide valuable insights into his thoughts and feelings during your interactions.     1. Frequency and Duration: Notice how often he laughs and how long his laughter lasts. If he frequently chuckles or has prolonged laughter during your conversations, it could indicate genuine enjoyment and interest in what you're saying.
