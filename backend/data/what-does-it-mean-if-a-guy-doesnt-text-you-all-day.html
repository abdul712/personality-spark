  • Limited Social Support: Without strong communication skills, individuals may struggle to build and maintain meaningful relationships, leading to a lack of social support.
  • Misinterpretation: Poor communication can result in misunderstandings and misinterpretations, creating barriers to effective social interaction.
  • Self-Isolation: Difficulty in communicating may lead individuals to isolate themselves, further perpetuating feelings of loneliness and alienation.
