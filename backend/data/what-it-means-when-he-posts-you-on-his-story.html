Communication Challenges Online

Online communication presents unique challenges that can affect the dynamics and overall quality of relationships. When it comes to posts on social media, these challenges become even more pronounced. Here are some of the ways in which online communication can impact relationships:

  • Misinterpretation: Text-based communication lacks the nuances of face-to-face interaction, leading to potential misunderstandings.
