    Tips for Maintaining and Styling White Nails

    To maintain and style white nails, it is important to follow a few key tips and techniques. Here are some tips for maintaining and styling white nails:

    1. Proper nail care:
    • Start by keeping your nails clean and well-trimmed.
    • Regularly file your nails to maintain their shape and prevent them from becoming brittle.
    • Use a gentle nail polish remover to remove any old polish or stains.
    1. Use a base coat:
    • Before applying white nail polish, it is essential to use a base coat.
    • This will create a smooth surface for the polish to adhere to and prevent staining of the nails.
    • It also helps to extend the life of your manicure.
    1. Apply thin coats:
    • When applying white nail polish, it is best to apply thin coats rather than thick ones.
    • This allows for better control and prevents streaking or clumping.
    • Allow each coat to dry completely before applying the next one.
